About Me

From 2008-2013, I helped organize a large annual neuroscience conference that took place in locations around the world. I was involved with creating and managing content for conference website and mobile app. This experience was my initial entry into the world of web development.

I transitioned my experience as a conference organizer to the position I currently hold as a Web Designer in the conference and event industry. I create websites, registration forms, mobile apps, surveys and emails for a wide variety of events, from large user conferences to small, corporate incentive trips.
