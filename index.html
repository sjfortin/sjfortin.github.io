About Me

From 2008-2013, I helped organize a large annual neuroscience conference. I was involved with creating and managing content for conference website and mobile app. This experience was my entry into the world of web development.

In 2013, I transitioned my experience as a conference organizer to a role as a Web Designer in the conference and event industry. I built websites, registration forms, mobile apps, surveys and emails for a wide variety of events, from large user conferences to corporate incentive trips.

Continuing my growth as a web developer, I am currently attending the Full Stack Engineering program at Prime Digital Academy.
